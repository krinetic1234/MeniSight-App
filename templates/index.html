About Us

MeniSight is a Machine Learning App that provides assistance for clinicians to fully evaluate their patient's tumor condition. We hope to eliminate subjectivity and bias in clinical decision making, as well as level the playing field between institutions.

Target Audience: Clinicians

MeniSight is a code-free tool that clincians can easily access and use when needed. Our machine learning models will make the predictions within less than 3 seconds. Note: only clinicians should use this app. Although patients are alowed to use this app, the primary audience is intended for experienced clincians and neurosurgeons.

Early Detection with CNNs

If you are unsure about your patient's tumor status, our Machine Learning models will predict the presence of a tumor within seconds upon input of an MRI image. It will use a combination of Convolutional Neural Networks and novel data augmentation techniques, and the model has an average prediction accuracy of around 98.6%, sensitivity of 97.4%, and specificity of 97.2%.

Survival Analysis and Prediction

If you are interested in determining the survival probability function for the patient, MeniSight will provide a substantive graph with key information regarding patient Overall Survival (OS). You must input the demographic and tumor information of the patient to receive an accurate prediction. The model is a Gradient Boosting Classifier with an average AUC of 0.798 and C-index of 0.759.

Functions

MeniSight provides two primary functions to help clinicians: detection and prognosis

Detection

Clicking on the icon or text above will take you to the detection page. You must input an MRI, and CNNs will be used to predict the presence of a tumor.

Prognosis

Clicking on the icon or text above will take you to the prognosis page. You must input demographic & tumor information before the Gradient Boosting model provides the survival function
